Rachel Verhaaren is an alumnus of the Maryland Institute College of Art in Baltimore, Maryland. She graduated Summa Cum Laude with a BFA in Photography and a minor in Art History. Rachel still lives in Baltimore and holds a varitey of positions with a clothing company and non-profit organization in Laurel, Maryland. She also participates in local theater.
